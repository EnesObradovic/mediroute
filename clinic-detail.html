About the Clinic

Established in 2009, Estepera Medical Centre is one of Istanbul's most trusted hair restoration and aesthetic surgery clinics. Accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI) — the gold standard in global healthcare — the centre has performed over 30,000 successful procedures for patients from the UK, Europe and beyond.

Our state-of-the-art facility spans 2,400 m², equipped with 6 advanced operating theatres, a private recovery suite, and a dedicated UK patient services team available 7 days a week. Every patient receives a personalised treatment plan and a native English-speaking coordinator from first enquiry through to their 12-month post-operative check-up.
